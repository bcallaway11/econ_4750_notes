Description of `rand_hie`

Source: This is a modified (mainly simplified) version of data from [https://jrnold.github.io/masteringmetrics/](https://jrnold.github.io/masteringmetrics/) for Mastering Metrics.  The original data comes from the RAND Health Insurance Experiment.

The data consists of 5,915 observations.

The following variables are included:

- person -- individual id
- meddol -- average medical expenditure over 5 years
- plantype -- what type of medical plan
- ftf -- average number of face to face medical visits over 5 years
- totadm -- average number hospital admissions over 5 years
- age -- individual age
- sex -- individual sex
